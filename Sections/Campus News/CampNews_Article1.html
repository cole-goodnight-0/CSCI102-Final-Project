Lady Gets locked in Stairwell

University Police at 1:06 p.m. Tuesday November 19th received a report of a woman who was unable to get out of an enclosed stairwell in Old Main. The woman shouted and made a banging noise to be let out and passers-by alerted University Police and 911. That call to 911 led to a number of calls of concern. The woman was able to get out of the stairwell, unharmed, on her own.
