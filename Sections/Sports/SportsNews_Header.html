Washington Nationals defeat the Houston Astros in Game 7 of the World Series

In the offseason earlier this year, the Washington Nationals traded star player Bryce Harper and the odds for them making the playoffs went down significantly. Battling the odds early in the season, they have a new young star Juan Soto that carried them on his back and brought them to the World Series. Beginning of the Season, the Houston Astros were the favorite to win the title, while the Nationals were just sitting out the top 10.
